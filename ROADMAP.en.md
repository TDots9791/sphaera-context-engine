# Roadmap

## Now · 1.0.0

- local context graph for code and documents;
- official online installer with signed-release verification;
- support for common AI tools;
- Trial, commercial, and research licenses.

## Next directions

- public integration examples and reproducible test suites;
- a compatibility catalog for platforms and formats;
- documentation for external adapters;
- a public source-availability policy.

## Influence the roadmap

Open an Issue describing the task, material to connect, and AI tool. Priority goes to repeatable scenarios with a clear outcome: less repeated reading, faster source verification, or more accurate review of changes.
